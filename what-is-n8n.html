What is n8n?

n8n (pronounced "n-eight-n") is a powerful, open-source workflow automation tool. It allows you to connect various applications, APIs, and services to automate tasks and build complex workflows without writing much code. Think of it as a bridge that brings your digital tools together.

  • Low-Code/No-Code: Build sophisticated automations with a visual drag-and-drop interface.
  • Extensible: Connect to hundreds of apps and services out-of-the-box.
  • Self-Hostable: Run it on your own server for full control and privacy.
  • Community Driven: A vibrant community provides support and new integrations.
