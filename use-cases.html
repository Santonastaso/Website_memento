Practical Use Cases

Automated Customer Support

Connect incoming customer queries from email or chat to an AI model. n8n can then route, summarize, or even draft responses, saving time and improving service.

Intelligent Content Curation

Scrape articles, use AI to summarize them, and then automatically post highlights to your social media or internal communication channels.

Automated Lead Qualification

Feed new lead data into an AI to assess their potential based on various criteria, then trigger follow-up actions in your CRM.

Dynamic Report Generation

Gather data from multiple sources, use AI to analyze trends and generate insights, and then compile automated reports.

Personalized Email Campaigns

Use AI to generate personalized email content for different customer segments, then send them via n8n-triggered campaigns.

Sentiment Analysis on Feedback

Automatically analyze customer reviews or social media comments for sentiment, and trigger alerts for negative feedback.
