n8n & Artificial Intelligence

The true power of n8n emerges when combined with Artificial Intelligence. n8n acts as the orchestrator, connecting your data and existing tools to cutting-edge AI models, including Large Language Models (LLMs) like Gemini.

This integration allows you to:

  • **Automate Content Generation:** Create articles, emails, social media posts.
  • **Enhance Data Processing:** Summarize text, extract entities, classify information.
  • **Build Smart Chatbots:** Connect user queries to AI for intelligent responses.
  • **Automate Image Generation:** Trigger AI image creation based on events.
